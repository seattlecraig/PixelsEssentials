PAGE 2
=====================================
HOME SYSTEM

Save locations and teleport back to them anytime.

Commands:
/home - List your homes
/home <n> - Teleport to home
/sethome - Set home named "home"
/sethome <n> - Set named home
/delhome <n> - Delete a home
=====================================
PAGE 3
=====================================
SETTING HOMES

Stand where you want your home, then type:

/sethome

This creates a home called "home".

For a custom name:
/sethome base
/sethome farm
/sethome mine

Names can use letters, numbers, underscores, and dashes.
=====================================
PAGE 4
=====================================
TELEPORTING HOME

To go to your default home:
/home home

To go to a named home:
/home farm

To see all your homes:
/home
or
/homes
=====================================
PAGE 5
=====================================
HOME INFO

To see coordinates of a home:
/homeinfo farm

This shows the world name and X, Y, Z location.

To see how many homes you have:
/homeinfo
=====================================
PAGE 6
=====================================
DELETING HOMES

To remove a saved home:

/delhome farm

The home is permanently deleted. You can create a new one with the same name later.
=====================================
PAGE 7
=====================================
BACK COMMAND

Returns you to your previous location.

After teleporting somewhere:
/back

This takes you back to where you were standing before.
=====================================
PAGE 8
=====================================
BACK AFTER DEATH

If you die, /back can return you to your death spot.

Simply type:
/back

Note: Returning to death locations may require special permission.
=====================================
PAGE 9
=====================================
REPAIR COMMANDS

Fix your damaged items!

/repair hand
Repairs the item you're holding.

/repair all
Repairs everything in your inventory and armor.
=====================================
PAGE 10
=====================================
REPAIR TIPS

- Only damaged items can be repaired (tools, weapons, armor)

- Items already at full durability show a message

- Repair all includes your hotbar, inventory, armor, and offhand
=====================================
PAGE 11
=====================================
AUTOFEED

Automatically keeps you fed!

/autofeed on
Enable autofeed

/autofeed off
Disable autofeed

/autofeed
Check current status
=====================================
PAGE 12
=====================================
HOW AUTOFEED WORKS

When enabled, your hunger bar is automatically restored when it gets low.

You never need to eat manually while autofeed is on!

Your setting is saved between sessions.
=====================================
PAGE 13
=====================================
DEATH PROTECTIONS

You may have special protections when you die:

Keep Inventory
Your items stay with you.

Keep XP
Your experience is preserved.

Keep Position
You respawn where you died.
=====================================

COMMAND SUMMARY

/sethome [name] - Save
/delhome <n> - Delete
/homeinfo [name] - Info
/back - Previous location
/repair hand - Fix held
/repair all - Fix all
